How to access Assistive Technology:

There are a number of public sector, charity sector, and private sector organisations that can help you find the right ATech for you.

There is sometimes a cost associated with acquiring ATech but there may be funding available or there may be free products or services that can meet your needs.

Local authority

Care Act 2014: This Act mandates that local authorities assess the needs of individuals for care and support, which can include the provision of assistive technology as part of the care package.
